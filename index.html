Long-term project

CLAW: an AI co-pilot ecosystem for genomics research.

CLAW is my long-term vision for a responsible, modular AI research co-pilot that helps genomics researchers move from raw outputs to clearer interpretation, documentation, and decision-making.

1

Interpretation layer

Explain GWAS, FUMA, UK Biobank, and ageing biology outputs in a structured, evidence-aware format.

2

Workflow layer

Turn repeatable research steps into transparent checklists, code templates, and reproducible reports.

3

Communication layer

Support scientific writing, presentations, and visual summaries without replacing researcher judgement.

4

Responsible AI layer

Keep assumptions visible, flag uncertainty, and make every AI-assisted decision reviewable by the researcher.
